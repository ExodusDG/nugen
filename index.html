What is NMVC?

NUGEN Metaverse Vegas City is a collection of parcels of LAND. This LAND is a finite, traversable, 3D virtual space within NMVC that is tokenized into NFT, having its smart contract on the robust X blockchain.

Each LAND is 16 meters by 16 meters, which is categorized as Basic and others as Exclusive.

These LANDs are segmented into parcels that are identified by Cartesian coordinates (X,Y).
